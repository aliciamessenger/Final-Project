Design

This design reflects the VT Shaped Student model. It has aspects that provide greater information for students on the various classes they could take to fulfill the T requirements of the model. The design also includes the V part of the model, in that it provides students various things for them to do in order to complete the V requirements.

The design also aids students who are unsure of what major/career they want. This is an important aspect of the design because it can be easy for undecided students to feel left behind. (MAKE THIS SOUND BETTER)
