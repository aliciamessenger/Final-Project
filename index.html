Introduction

My name is Pam Beasley and I am a sophomore at Virginia Tech. I'm an English major, specifically Literature and Language. I'm also minoring in Spanish. I took a Gap Year after high school and traveled around the world. I don't know what I want to do career wise in the future, but I know I want to travel as much as I can. I'm also a huge fan of memes.

Before this class, posting on Facebook and tweeting was pretty much the extent of my digital writing experience. I hope to have an understanding of basic HTML and CSS, and website creating and digital editing. Hopefully my strong foundation will give me the experience I need if I'm to apply those skills at a future job.

So far I'm enjoying Writing and Digital Media. I'm hoping that by the end of the semester I'll have a better idea of if I want to continue down the digital writing path and make it one of my focuses, or if I want to explore something else.
